Data collection

The extension does not collect or transmit any information, personal or otherwise.

The extension's settings are stored only on your local machine, and are never transmitted anywhere.

This extension does not contain any adverts or tracking components.

How does it work?

The extension works by scanning pages you visit for mailto: links, and converting those it finds to point directly to a Gmail (or Google Apps mail) compose window. It operates solely within your browser, and does not require sending or receiving any information.
